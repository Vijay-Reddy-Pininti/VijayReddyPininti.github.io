Enhancing Road Safety Through Predictive Modelling of Motor Vehicle Collisions

This project aims to develop a predictive model using a motor vehicle collision dataset to accurately assess collision severity based on various contributing factors. The objectives include identifying key factors influencing crashes, pinpointing high-risk accident locations, analyzing the impact of driving behaviors on collision severity, investigating the relationship between vehicle types and collision severity, and assessing the significance of the time of day in influencing crash severity. Ultimately, this research will contribute valuable insights for enhancing road safety and accident prevention strategies.
Python, Numpy, Pandas, Scikitlearn, Seaborn, Matplot

Logistic Regression From Scratch

Implementing logistic regression from scratch on the penguins dataset involves statistically modeling the probability that a penguin belongs to a particular gender based on its features, using a sigmoid function for prediction, and optimizing the model coefficients through gradient descent to minimize the prediction error.
